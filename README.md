## What to Know Before You Buy

A few honest notes based on community feedback:

**Payment processing**: XSX accepts PayPal and Alipay, with more recent Stripe Express support. There was a known bug with their Stripe implementation in mid-2025 (since patched), so if you run into any payment confirmation issues, submitting a support ticket directly through their client area is the fastest resolution path.

**Support channels**: Support is handled via their ticket system. Pre-sales contact is primarily through the open ticket form on their site. Response times vary — for straightforward technical issues on established accounts this tends to work fine, though turnaround isn't instant.

**Self-managed**: These are unmanaged servers. You're getting root access and a reboot button — not hand-holding. If you're comfortable with Linux and SSH, that's all you need.

**Refund policy**: XSX does have a stated refund policy, so check the terms on their site before ordering if that's important to you.
